== Description ==

Customize and promote your events, products and offers on your WordPress site with the easy-to-use PopUp Banner Plugin by CreativeMinds. You can add a banner across your whole site or on specific pages and advertise your upcoming specials in the middle or bottom of the page. With the Pro Version, customize the look and timing of these popups and test the success of your campaign.

**Plugin usage instructions**

* 1) Go to "Add New Campaign"
* 2) Fill the "Title" of the campaign and "content" of one or many Advertisement Items
* 3) Click "Add Advertisement Item" to dynamically add more items
* 4) Check "Show on every page"
* 5) Click "Save Changes"
* 6) Go to any page of your website
* 7) Watch the banner with Advertisement Item
* 8) Close the banner clicking "X" icon

== Installation ==

1. Upload `cm-pop-up-banners-for-wordpress` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
